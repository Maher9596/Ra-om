About Raom

Ra’om is an Arabic word used for describing the heart of a mother. It is also used for describing a kind heart.

Ra’om focuses on supporting parents to develop goals management mindset in their children in a fun and practical way. The ways may differ, but at some point most parents had used a tool (it could be a white board or a paper on the fridge) to motivate their kids toward improving their behavior or learning.

We tried to make the process more organized and scientific through introducing the concept of goals management and it’s importance as a skill for kids to gain.
